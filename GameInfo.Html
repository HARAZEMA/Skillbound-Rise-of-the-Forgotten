Gameplay Overview

In Skillbound, you explore dangerous dungeons, fight monsters, and collect rewards. Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and defeat powerful bosses to become a legend.

Players can earn:

    Game Features

    Gold Coins

    Earned from: defeating monsters, selling loot, completing simple quests. Uses: potions, basic weapons, Pay training fees at the dojo, Common, easy to grind.

    Spirit Crystals

    Earned from: boss fights or hidden chest Uses: Unlock advanced Aura evolutions,Mid-tier, more valuable than gold.

    Divine Essence

    Earned from: defeating world bosses, or purchased with Robux. Uses: Unlock hidden “Auraless powers.”, Re-roll your Aura type (get a new divine Aura), Access exclusive legendary weapons, skins, and cosmetics, Super rare, prestige-level.
